Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Ronnie Rivera and Carla Rivera, R&C Airmakers Engineering Services was established with one mission—to provide reliable, honest, and professional HVAC solutions. Built on over 13 years of industry experience, we continue to serve residential and commercial clients with quality workmanship, dependable service, and lasting customer relationships.

  • Professional HVAC Technicians
  • Quality Workmanship Guaranteed
  • Honest & Transparent Pricing
  • Reliable After-Sales Support

R&C Airmakers Engineering Services provides professional air conditioning installation, cleaning, maintenance, troubleshooting, repair, and commercial HVAC solutions throughout Bulacan and nearby areas.
